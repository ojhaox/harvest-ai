Getting Started

What is Harvest AI?

Harvest AI is an automated trading platform that monitors Key Opinion Leaders (KOLs) in the Solana ecosystem and executes trades based on their social media activity.

Quick Start Guide

  1. Connect your wallet (Phantom or MetaMask)
  2. Add KOLs to your tracking list
  3. Configure your trading parameters
  4. Start automated trading

Features Guide

KOL Tracking

Monitor influential crypto personalities and their market insights in real-time.

  • Token mention detection
  • Automated trading signals

AI Trading

Our AI analyzes KOL activity and executes trades automatically based on:

  • Tweet sentiment
  • Market conditions
  • Token momentum
  • Historical performance

FAQs

How does the AI analyze tweets?

Our AI uses natural language processing to analyze sentiment, context, and token mentions in real-time.

Is my wallet safe?

Yes, we never store your private keys. All transactions require your explicit approval.

What are the trading fees?

Standard network fees apply. Harvest AI charges 1% on profitable trades only.

Can I customize trading strategies?

Yes, you can adjust risk levels, trade sizes, and AI sensitivity in the Settings panel.
